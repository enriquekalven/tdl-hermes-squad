## 👥 The 6-Role Matrix

1. **10X Lead (Originate · Innovate · Shape)**
   - Shapes the vertical narrative and qualifies high-leverage opportunities moving client EBITDA.
2. **AI Activation Lead - AIAL (Govern · De-risk · Expand)**
   - Program governance, executive alignment, client sign-offs, and velocity protection.
3. **Technical Deployment Lead - TDL (Discover · Redesign · Prototype)**
   - Immerses into client domain, redesigns legacy workflows into agentic pipelines, writes PRDs, builds low-fi prototypes, and defines eval criteria.
4. **Forward-Deployed Engineer - FDE (Build · Integrate · Operate)**
   - Anchors prototypes in client environment, hardens enterprise integrations (SSO, ETL, IAM), and builds automated evaluation pipelines.
5. **Platform Engineer (Productize · Accelerate · Scale)**
   - Builds "Golden Paths," reusable agent components, and CI/CD observability patterns.
6. **Agentic Transformation Lead - ATL (Transform · Scale · Enable)**
   - Organizational change management (OCM), staff enablement, and driving adoption across client business units.
