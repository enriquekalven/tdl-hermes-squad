## 📉 Slide 2: Level 1 — The Manual / Intuition-Led Approach
# Level 1: Relying Purely on Manual Experience

### 🛠️ How It Works:
- TDLs and FDEs rely on personal memory, unstructured Google Docs, sticky notes, and ad-hoc Slack/Chat threads.
- Requirements and scope are held in team members' heads.

### 🛑 Load-Bearing Pain Points:
- ❌ **30-50% Context Tax**: Team spends hours re-explaining legacy decisions to new engineers.
- ❌ **Uncontrolled Scope Drift**: SOWs balloon from 12 weeks to 24 weeks when client executives demand mid-flight changes.
- ❌ **Non-Standardized Quality**: Output quality varies wildly depending on individual TDL seniority.
- ❌ **Zero Scalability**: Scaling delivery requires hiring linearly.

---

## 💡 Slide 3: Level 2 — Skill-Assisted Execution
# Level 2: Ad-Hoc AI Skills & Prompt Tooling

### 🛠️ How It Works:
- TDLs adopt individual AI prompts or specialized skills (`create-prd`, `threat-model-analyst`, `test-driven-development`).
- Agents generate initial drafts of PRDs or code snippets on demand.

### ⚡ Progress Made:
- ⚡ **2x Speedup on Artifact Generation**: Rapid drafting of PRDs and unit tests.

### 🛑 Remaining Bottlenecks:
- ❌ **Session Amnesia**: Chatbots forget context as soon as the session closes; TDLs must manually copy/paste context.
- ❌ **Fragmented Execution**: No overarching process linking discovery to production deployment.
- ❌ **No Scope Enforcement**: Prompts don't protect sprint commitments against scope bloat.

---

## 🛠️ Slide 4: Level 3 — Meta-Skills & Phase-Gated Toolbox
# Level 3: Structured Meta-Skills & Dynamic Capability Slots

### 🛠️ How It Works:
- TDLs trigger a unified operational meta-skill (`tdl-field-guide`) governing the 12-week transformation lifecycle.
- **Dynamic Capability Slots**: Standardizes tool resolution per phase (`#CAPABILITY: PRD-Creation` ➔ `create-prd`).

```
Phase 1: Discover (Weeks 0-2)  ──► Phase 2: Architect (Weeks 3-6) ──► Phase 3: Build (Weeks 6-10) ──► Phase 4: Launch (Weeks 11-12)
 (Intake & Baseline KPIs)           (ADRs & Threat Modeling)          (TDD & 5-Axis Code Review)        (Evals & EBITDA Shift Report)
```

### ⚡ Progress Made:
- ⚡ **Standardized 12-Week Playbook**: Consistent SOW execution across client accounts.
- ⚡ **Scope Protection**: Dynamic capability mapping keeps deliverables aligned with phase goals.

### 🛑 Remaining Bottlenecks:
- ❌ **Single-Agent Bottleneck**: One generalist agent handles context, coding, security, and financial modeling.
- ❌ **Ephemeral Memory**: Lacks durable long-term storage across team members.
